The Weekend is here.

The Weekend is a popular musician who makes pop music. He played at the super bowl halftime show in 2021. He's from Canada and has 4 albums. His most recent album is after hours which released on march 20,2020.

The history of The Weekend

The Weekend was born in Toronto Canada. His parents split up when he was young and didnt see his dad often. This caused him to start doing drugs at a young age. He dropped out of high school which is what inspired his stage name. He first made music and uploaded it through YouTube in 2010 with a producer. His first three songs were "What You Need", "loft Music", and "The Morning". He would release more music over the years but the album that would make him famous was Beauty Behind the Madness.

The Weekends rise to fame

The Weekend released Beauty Behind the Madness in 2015. This album released popular songs like "The Hills", and "Cant Fell my Face". These two songs were on the billboard 100 which is a big deal. A year later he would release "Star Boy" with Daft Punk. Over the years he would release more albums and perform on snl. in early 2020 he released his newest album "After Hours".
